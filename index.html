Global Heritage

Saffron is known as “Kesar” (Hindi) and “Zafran” (Urdu, Persian, Arabic) — reflecting its deep cultural roots across regions.

Hindi · Kesar (केसर)

Urdu · Zafran (زعفران)

How to Use

Crush a small pinch of saffron threads and soak in warm water or milk for 10–15 minutes before adding to your dish. This releases its full color, aroma, and flavor.
