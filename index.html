The Azaad Promise
Why Azaad Exists

Built by someone who
grew up with this.

Azaad was founded in Southern California. Saffron is an ingredient that matters deeply to the communities here — Persian, Indian, Afghan, Middle Eastern households where it appears in rice, in tea, in desserts, in celebrations. It is not a specialty item. It is a staple. And yet finding a reliable, high-quality source locally is genuinely difficult.

Most of what is available on local shelves is either overpriced with no documentation behind the claim, or cheap and of questionable quality. The gap between what people were paying for and what they were actually getting was the reason Azaad exists.

"The communities that use saffron most deserve to know exactly what is in their jar. That is the only reason Azaad was built."

No embellishment. No family heritage story. Just a simple observation that a product people care about deeply was hard to source with any confidence — and that problem was worth solving honestly.
