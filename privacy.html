Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026 · Applies to azaadsaffron.com
Azaad Saffron is built on one principle — you deserve to know exactly what you are getting. That applies to what is in your jar and to how we handle your information. This policy tells you both, plainly.

Who We Are

Azaad Saffron operates azaadsaffron.com, a brand website for premium certified saffron based in California. References to "we," "us," or "Azaad" in this policy refer to Azaad Saffron and its owner.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate this website and communicate with you. This currently includes:

  • Email address — if you subscribe to our launch notification list via the form on our website. This is entirely voluntary. We use Mailchimp (Intuit Inc.) to manage our subscriber list. When you subscribe, your email address is transmitted to and stored on Mailchimp's servers in accordance with their Privacy Policy (mailchimp.com/legal/privacy). Mailchimp's privacy policy applies to how they store and process this data.
  • Contact information you voluntarily provide — such as your name and email address if you contact us directly via Instagram or any future contact form.
  • Usage data — standard information collected automatically when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site. This is collected through standard web hosting logs.
  • Communications — if you send us a message via Instagram or email, we retain that correspondence to respond to your enquiry.

We do not currently operate an e-commerce store. We do not collect payment information, shipping addresses, or order history at this time.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to:

  • Respond to enquiries and messages you send us directly.
  • Notify you of product launches, farmers market dates, or brand updates if you have opted in to receive communications.
  • Understand how visitors use our website so we can improve it.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling. We do not send unsolicited marketing communications.

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not sell, rent, or trade your email address or any other personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

We share subscriber email addresses with Mailchimp solely to deliver the communications you opted into. This is a service provider relationship — Mailchimp processes data on our behalf and is contractually prohibited from using your data for their own marketing purposes.

To opt out of our email list at any time, click the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email we send you, or contact us via @azaadsaffron on Instagram and we will remove you within 48 hours.

California residents may also exercise their rights under CCPA as described in the section below.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Full stop.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — companies that help us operate our website, such as our web hosting provider (GitHub) and domain registrar (GoDaddy). These providers access information only as necessary to provide their services and are bound by their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or government authority, we may disclose information as legally required.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not currently use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics. Standard web server logs may capture basic visit information as described above. We do not use cookies to track your activity across other websites.

California Residents — Your Rights Under CCPA

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information. These rights include:

  • The right to know — you may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the sources of that information, the purposes for which we use it, and any third parties with whom we share it.
  • The right to delete — you may request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions permitted by law.
  • The right to opt out of sale — we do not sell personal information. There is nothing to opt out of.
  • The right to non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights. Exercising these rights will have no effect on the service or experience we provide you.
  • The right to correct — you may request that we correct inaccurate personal information we hold about you.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via Instagram at @azaadsaffron. We will respond to verified requests within 45 days as required by California law. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

California residents may also contact the California Attorney General's office at oag.ca.gov/privacy if you have concerns about our privacy practices.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. If you contact us and subsequently request deletion of your information, we will remove it from our records within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately via @azaadsaffron on Instagram and we will delete it promptly.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we hold. Our website is served over HTTPS. We do not store sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary for communications. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Links to Other Sites

Our website contains a link to our Instagram account (@azaadsaffron). Instagram's privacy practices are governed by Meta's Privacy Policy, which is separate from this document. We encourage you to review it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our business grows and our data practices evolve. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, requests to exercise your California rights, or concerns about how we handle your information, please contact us via Instagram at @azaadsaffron. We will respond promptly.
